Cold Call Scripts
These are guides, not rigid lines. Sound like a human. The goal of every call: book a 15-minute discovery call with Surya.
Before calling: look at their Google Maps listing, website, reviews, last activity. Know something about them before you dial.
1Opening — No Website
▾
You say:
"Hey, is this [Business Name]? Great — I'm [Your Name], I work with SuryaWebStudio. I was just looking you up on Google and noticed you don't have a website yet. Is that right?"
Wait for them to confirm. Then:
"Yeah, so we build websites specifically for local businesses like yours — something simple that helps customers find you and contact you directly. We've done a few in [their niche or area]. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to see if it makes sense for you?"
⚡ Ask if they have a website first. Let them confirm the problem themselves. Then offer the call, not the service.
2Opening — Outdated Website
▾
You say:
"Hi, is this [Business Name]? Hey — I'm [Your Name] from SuryaWebStudio. I came across your website and it looks like it might not have been updated in a while. Are you still using it to get clients?"
After they respond:
"Yeah, so a lot of businesses in [their area/niche] are losing customers because their site looks outdated or doesn't work on mobile. We redesign and rebuild those — do you have 15 minutes sometime this week to take a look together?"
⚡ Don't say "your site is bad." Say it looks like it hasn't been updated. Less offensive, same point.
3Opening — SMM Pitch
▾
You say:
"Hi, this is [Your Name] from SuryaWebStudio. I came across [Business Name] on Instagram — looks like you haven't posted in a while. Are you still active on social?"
After they respond:
"Got it. So we manage social media for local businesses — we handle all the content, posting, everything — so you don't have to think about it. It's helped a few businesses in [niche] get more consistent enquiries. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to see if it fits?"
4Handling Objections
▾
Objection: "I'm not interested."
Too quick — they haven't heard anything yet.
"No worries at all — I'm not trying to sell you anything right now. I just wanted to know if getting more customers online is even something on your radar. If not, totally fine."
Objection: "How much does it cost?"
Don't give a number. Push to the call.
"It depends on what you actually need — that's why I want to set up the short call. But for small businesses, it's usually pretty affordable. The call itself is free, no commitment."
Objection: "I already have someone handling this."
"That's great — if it's working well, no reason to change anything. I just figured I'd reach out since [reason you spotted an issue]. If you're happy, no worries."
Objection: "Send me an email."
"For sure, I'll send something over. Just so I send the right info — what's the main thing you'd want to improve right now: getting more website traffic, or more enquiries from social media?"
⚡ "Send me an email" usually means soft no. Get info first, then send a tailored email.

Outreach Process
Follow this exact sequence every day. Consistency beats intensity.

Daily Workflow

01
Get Your Lead List from Surya
Surya sends a batch of leads — each has business name, contact info, and the specific issue. Read through before reaching out.
02
Understand the Lead Before Contacting
Take 60 seconds to look at the business. What do they do? Where are they? This is what lets you personalise and not sound like a bot.
03
Call First, Email After
Cold call is the highest conversion method. If no answer or they say "send an email," send the email within 1 hour.
04
Log Every Attempt
Record lead name, date, method (call/email/DM), and outcome — same day, every time. No log = no commission.
05
Follow Up Once
No email reply in 3–4 days? Send follow-up once. No DM reply in 2–3 days? One follow-up. Then mark "no response" and move on.
06
Book the Call — Not the Sale
The moment someone agrees, send Surya's calendar link, lock in a time, log it as "booked," and notify Surya on WhatsApp immediately.

Rules & Standards
Non-negotiable. Breaking these will affect your position.

✅ Always Do This

  • Personalise every outreach — use the business name and reference the specific issue Surya flagged
  • Log every contact attempt and outcome in the tracking sheet, same day
  • Contact every lead Surya sends — no skipping ones that "look hard"
  • Be honest about what we do — don't overpromise or make up results
  • Sound human on calls — read the script but don't recite it robotically
  • Notify Surya on WhatsApp the same day a call is booked

🚫 Never Do This

  • Never quote a final price — say "depends on the project, we'll go over it on the call"
  • Never send copy-paste mass emails with zero personalisation
  • Never DM the same person more than twice
  • Never make guarantees ("we'll get you 10 clients") — only promise a conversation
  • Never represent yourself as the owner or founder
  • Never skip logging leads — untracked leads don't count toward commission
